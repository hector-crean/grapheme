Mania
Symptoms & Alterations

: A distinct period of 1 week or more (less if hospitalization is required) during which patients experience abnormally and persistently raised, expansive, or irritable mood. The mood disturbance and increased energy or activity must be accompanied by ≥3 additional symptoms listed below. If the mood is irritable (rather than elevated or expansive), at least 4 of the below symptoms must be present.
