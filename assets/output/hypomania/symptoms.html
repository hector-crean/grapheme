Hypomania
Symptoms

: Distinct period of abnormally and persistently elevated, expansive, or irritable mood and abnormally and persistently increased activity or energy, lasting ≥4 days, not sufficiently severe to cause pronounced impairment in social or occupational functioning or to necessitate hospitalization.
