Treatment
Effect of Treatments

The symptoms of bipolar disorder can be treated through different types of pharmacological medication such as mood stabilibizers like lithium, antidepressants and antipsychotics. Different treatment avenues result in changes in different regions of the brain.

Click on each region to learn more about how they are affected by specific treatments.
