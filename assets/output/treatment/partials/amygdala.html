Amygdala

Mood stabilizers:

Lithium

Lithium has been associated with an increase in amygdala volume, a brain region implicated in emotional processing and cognitive control suggesting that lithium has considerable neurotrophic effects.

Anti-epileptic

Increased amygdala volume in patients taking anti-epileptics compared to those patients who did not.

Antidepressants

An association between decreased right amygdala volume and antidepressants have been reported in bipolar patients.

Antipsychotics

A decrease in the right amygdala volume has been associated with antipsychotic use, suggesting this may be a proxy for more severe bipolar illness.
