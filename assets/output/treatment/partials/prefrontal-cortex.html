Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)

Mood stabilizers:

Lithium

Lithium has been associated with an increase in prefrontal cortical volume, which has been observed even over a 4 week treatment period. Lithium has also been shown to increase orbitofrontal cortical volume, brain regions which are involved in emotion and memory.

Anticonvulsant

Larger orbitofrontal cortex has been reported in bipolar patients taking anticonvulsants compared to bipolar patients that were not.

Antipsychotics

An increase in medial prefrontal cortical volume has been associated with antipsychotics.
