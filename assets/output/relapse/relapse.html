Relapse

Definition in terms of1:

  1. : In the context of manic episodes, relapse is defined as occurring ≤4 weeks after remission from the acute episode
  2. : In the context of depressive episodes, relapse is defined as occurring ≤8 weeks after remission from the acute episode.

Statistics2:

Predictors of time to relapse3:

History of alcoholism
Psychotic features during index episode
Occupational status
Depressive symptoms during index episode
