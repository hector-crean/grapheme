  • Relative to matched controls, BD patients show decreased thickness in cortical regions of the brain which are associated with cognitive control and behavior regulation.
  • There was a correlation between the duration of illness and the level of cortical thickness in frontal, temporal, occipital and parietal regions.
  • Females had less cortical thinning than males.
